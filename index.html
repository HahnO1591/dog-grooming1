SERVICES

We Provide Better

Bathing

Cleaning the pet's coat using appropriate shampoos and conditioners. It helps remove dirt, odors, and loose hair while maintaining the overall cleanliness of the pet.

Haircut/Trimming

Groomers provide haircuts or trims based on the pet owner's preferences and the specific breed's grooming standards. They use various techniques and tools, such as clippers, scissors, and thinning shears, to achieve the desired style.

Teeth Cleaning

involve brushing the pet's teeth or using special dental products to promote oral hygiene.

Nail Trimming

Trim the pet's nails to an appropriate length, avoiding overgrowth and potential discomfort. They may use nail clippers or grinders, ensuring the process is safe and doesn't harm the pet.
